What is SensorWeb?

SensorWeb is an open data platform for IoT. Share data from IoT devices without coding. Our platform allows you to create REST APIs through a Web interface.

Using our open cloud service, you can not only solve problems that affect you and your organization, but, if you choose, you can share your data with the global community to solve problems that affect everyone.
